FAQ

What others ask

Why should I join the community?

The community is a great place to surround yourself with passionate designers looking to level up, connect with likeminded others, and get feedback on your work.

How often do you run live sessions?

We host a live session at least once per month.

Are live sessions recorded?

Yes. All live sessions are recorded and made available in the community.

Will I have any career support?

Yes, you'll be able to book one free session with Fons as part of your subscription, as well as additional discounted sessions. Besides that, you'll have access to the community discussion board to get feedback and learn from others.

How do I manage my subscription?

Once signed up, you are able to log in, and manage your subscription details in your profile settings.
